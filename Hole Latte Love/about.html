About Us

Hole Latte Love was created by Colette Blechert. While living in Baltimore, Maryland, Colette's enthusiasm for donuts grew as she made it a goal to find small business donut shops in the towns she would visit. She finally opened the doors of hole latte love in 2021 which showcases the favorite aspects of the donut shops she found throughout the US and brings them to Brigham City, Utah.

Colette is married to her wonderful husband, Jordan. They have 3 beautiful children and enjoy sharing their love of sweets with them.

Her favorite donut is chocolate frosted with rainbow sprinkles. She continues to try more flavor combos to help expand Hole Latte Love's growing selection.
